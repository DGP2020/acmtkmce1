About ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, providing resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM TKMCE is a student chapter committed to fostering learning, leadership, and innovation among students in the field of computer science.

Learn More

Founded in 1947, the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) has played a pivotal role in shaping the field of computing. With a global network of over 100,000 members, ACM promotes dialogue, sharing of resources, and professional development across academia, industry, and students. Its publications, conferences, and educational resources have been at the forefront of innovation for decades.

Our Vision

To be a premier student chapter that empowers young minds in computing, promoting ethical practices, interdisciplinary collaboration, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

  • To foster interest and excellence in computer science and related domains.
  • To organize workshops, talks, and competitions to enhance technical skills.
  • To build a supportive community of aspiring tech professionals.
